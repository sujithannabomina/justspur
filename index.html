FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What are Spurs exactly?
A Spur is a right-swipe on JustSpur — your intentional "I'm interested" signal. You get 5 per day on the free plan. They reset automatically every morning at midnight so you always have a fresh start.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime from the app settings or by emailing support@justspur.com. You keep premium access until the end of your current billing period. No fees, no questions asked.
How does activity-based matching work?
When you sign up, you choose 2 date categories (up to 8 on premium). You'll only see profiles of people who share at least one category with you — AND who match your gender preferences AND are within 30 miles. When you both swipe right, it's a match.
Which cities is JustSpur launching in?
We're launching in Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, Pune, and Hyderabad first — then expanding city by city to ensure quality matches everywhere we go. Join the waitlist and we'll tell you when your city is live.
How do you keep profiles safe and real?
Every user signs in with Google (no anonymous accounts possible). We have easy report and block tools, a dedicated moderation team, and photo verification coming very soon. Safety is built into the foundation, not bolted on afterwards.
Is the 30-mile radius fixed?
Currently yes, though we're developing a smart radius system that adapts based on user density in your area — smaller in dense metros like Mumbai, slightly larger in smaller cities. This is coming in a future update.
When does the app launch on Play Store?
Very soon! We're in the final stages of development. Join the waitlist on our homepage and you'll be among the first to know — plus get early access perks when we launch.

Our Story

We got tired of
going nowhere.

We built JustSpur because we were sick of matching with someone, texting for three weeks, and never actually meeting. Dating apps had quietly turned into entertainment products — not tools for genuine connection.

The problem wasn't the people. It was the system. When swiping is infinite and free, nobody takes it seriously. When there's no shared intention about what kind of date you're looking for, every conversation starts from scratch.

So we stripped it all back. Pick your date type. Use your 5 daily Spurs with intention. Meet someone nearby who wants the same thing. That's the whole idea. That's JustSpur.
